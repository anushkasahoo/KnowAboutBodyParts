How Our Body Works

Our body is composed of various organs that allows our body to work. These organs are of great importance as each organ has its own function to perform. The parts of our body do not work individually, instead, each part functions day and night supported by all the others organs.

The organs in the body are of two categories : External Organs and Internal Organs


External Organs

The organs of the body that are located on the surface of the body are called External Organs. These organs can be seen and touched. Eg - Nose, Ear, Eyes etc.

Skin is the largest external organs of our body.


Internal Organs

The organs of the body that are located inside of the body are called Internal Organs. These organs cannot be seen and touched. Eg - Heart, Brain, Stomach etc.

Small Intestine is the largest external organs of our body.
