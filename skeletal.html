The Skeletal System

The skeletal system is made up of the Skeletal Organs.The framework of bones make up the skeletal of our body. The skeletal system includes :


Skull

The skull is a bony structure that supports the face and forms a protective cavity for the brain. It is made up of 22 bones.

Skull can indicate a person's life history origin.


Backbone

The backbone is made up of 33 bones. These bones are called vertebrae. The vertebrae , together forms the spinal column. The vertebrae forms a long flexible tissue so its allows to bend forward , backward and sideways.


Ribcage

A Rib cage is a bony structure which is attached to the vertebral column and sternum in humans and it is helpful in the protection of heart and lungs.

With every breath we take in ,our ribcage expands by 3 to 5 cm and gets back normal as we release breath.


Limbs

Limbs are the joints that allow the movements. We have two types of limbs Upper limbs and Lower limbs. The Upper limbs and Lower limbs are commonly called arms and legs. Limbs help us in many type of movements like walking, writing, jumping etc.

A knee consists of two seperate joints.
