The Muscular System

The muscular system consists of various types of muscle that each play a crucial role in the function of the body. Muscles allow a person to move, speak, and chew. They control heartbeat, breathing, and digestion.


Voluntary Muscles

These muscles are under humans control. Eg - Hands, legs, neck etc.

Involuntary Muscles

These muscles are not under humans control. Eg - Heart, eyes, lungs etc.


Cardiac Muscles

Our heart consists of these special kind of muscle. They are self-contracting and help in pumping of blood.
